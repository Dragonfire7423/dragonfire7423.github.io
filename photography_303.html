I've been practicing my photography skills in order to be able to use those skills in a portfolio. The vast majority of my photos are of those in nature because I feel like those are the easiest to take. But when I can, my friend Ani does a good job at being a model for the photos. Allows us to make memories while I can also practice my skills with a camera.

The majority of my photos are taken from my iPhone SE 2nd Gen. I practice having layers in my photos, such as foreground, middlegorund, and background. I also practice putting the interesting subjects in the thirds on a grid and finding cool places to take photos in the first place. I've been having fun putting to practice all the small tips I've learned this past year.
