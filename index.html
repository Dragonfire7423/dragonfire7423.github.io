Overwatch Metas

A brief summary of popular metas in the game, Overwatch



Overwatch is a team-based multiplayer first-person shooter game. Referred to as a hero shooter, the game assigns players into a 6v6 format with each player allowed to pick a character with unique abilites. Those heroes, when paired with other heroes, can create synergy and form what is called a meta. A meta is a strategy agreed upon by the community that has the best chance/most optimal way of winning. Below is a list of popular metas and descriptions of why certain metas worked well.
