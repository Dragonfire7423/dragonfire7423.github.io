Overwatch Metas

A brief summary of popular metas in the game, Overwatch


Overwatch is a team-based multiplayer first-person shooter game. Referred to as a hero shooter, the game assigns players into a 6v6 format with each player allowed to pick a character with unique abilites. Those heroes, when paired with other heroes, can create synergy and form what is called a meta. A meta is a strategy agreed upon by the community that has the best chance/most optimal way of winning. Meta descriptions

Beyblade

The Beyblade meta comes from a Reaper player using their "Death Blossom" ultimate while boosted by an Ana player's ultimate, "Nano Boost". Effective at eliminating groups of enemy players.

GOATS

The GOATS meta is seen as the most oppresive meta to ever come to Overwatch. Consisting of primarily of Reinhardt, Zarya, Brigitte, Lucio, and D.Va, the composition focused on moving fast and doing overwhelming amounts of damage while rapid healing your own team. No previous meta could hold up to the aggressiveness of this new meta and it quickly took over.
